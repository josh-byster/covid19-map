COVID-19
An interactive outbreak timeline by Josh Byster.

For most of us, the previous few weeks have been full of uncertainty in multiple facets. Daily life has been drastically affected. At the time of this writing, COVID-19 has claimed the lives of thousands, and new information is emerging each day.

It can be a challenge to conceptualize the exponential nature of the spread. Hence, I created this map to help the community track the evolution of the novel Coronavirus on both a global and local scale.

My motivation for the creation of this map is not to incite fear, but rather emphasize the importance of social distancing, and how we, as a community, have the power to "flatten the curve" by working together.
