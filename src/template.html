COVID-19
An interactive outbreak timeline by Josh Byster.

For most of us, the previous few weeks have been full of uncertainty in multiple facets. Many lives have been drastically affected. At the time of this writing, COVID-19 has claimed the lives of thousands, and new information is emerging each day.

It can be a challenge to conceptualize the exponential nature of the spread. Hence, I created this map to help the community track the evolution of the novel Coronavirus on both a global and local scale.

I hope this map, rather than inciting fear, emphasizes the importance of social distancing, and how we, as a community, have the power to "flatten the curve".

This project was created with d3.js and the code is available on GitHub.

Data source: Johns Hopkins University: The Center for Systems Science and Engineering

Design inspired by Andrew Munsell's Ebola Visualization.
